Social Distancing Violation Detector

via fine-tuned YOLO v3 & Computer vision

The novel coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) has brought global crisis with its deadly spread. The absence of any active therapeutic agents and the lack of immunity against COVID19 increases the vulnerability of the population. Since there are no vaccines available, “Social Distancing” is the only feasible approach to fight against this pandemic. Motivated by this notion, this project proposes a deep-learning based framework for automating the task of monitoring social distancing by using YOLO v3 and Computer vision concepts.
